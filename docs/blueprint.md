# **App Name**: LumiSkin

## Core Features:

- Photo Upload: Allow users to upload photos of their skin for analysis.
- Skin Analysis: Analyze uploaded photos to assess skin type, conditions, and concerns using AI tool to help determine when additional outside data sources need to be consulted.
- Product Recommendation: Provide personalized product recommendations based on the skin analysis results.
- Progress Tracking: Enable users to track their skin's progress over time by comparing past and present photos.
- Favorite Products: Allow users to save their favorite products for later reference.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft green (#B2D7B2) to convey a sense of natural and healthy skin.
- Background color: Very light green (#E5EBE5), a desaturated tint of the primary color.
- Accent color: Light peach (#FFDAB9) to provide a warm and inviting contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font with an elegant and intellectual feel.
- Use clean, minimalist icons to represent different skin types, concerns, and product categories.
- Incorporate subtle animations when transitioning between different sections or displaying analysis results.
